Our Story

Two Decades.
One Standard.

Founded in Kota in 2004, Meridian Build Group grew from a two-man civil contracting firm into one of Rajasthan's most trusted construction groups — without ever compromising on quality.

2004

Founded in Kota

Vikram Rathore and Suresh Jain register Meridian Build Group with a single JCB and a vision to build better. First project: a residential compound in Kota for a local developer.

2008

First Crore-Plus Contract

Secured a ₹ 4.2 Cr school campus project from the Rajasthan government — our entry into the institutional construction sector.

2012

Infrastructure Division Launched

Expanded into civil infrastructure with our first road project. Hired 60 additional engineers and site managers. Revenue crossed ₹ 50 Cr.

2016

ISO Certifications & Green Mandate

Achieved ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001 certifications. Committed to GRIHA-aligned practices on all new commercial projects going forward.

2020

₹ 500 Cr Revenue Milestone

Crossed cumulative project value of ₹ 500 Cr despite the challenging pandemic year. Delivered the Kota Water Treatment Plant — our single largest project to date.

2024

Today: 180+ Projects, 12 States

Active across Rajasthan, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh and beyond. 850 employees on payroll. And still the same founding philosophy: do the work right, or don't do it at all.

"We didn't set out to be the biggest construction company in Rajasthan. We set out to be the most trusted."
— Vikram Rathore, Co-Founder
